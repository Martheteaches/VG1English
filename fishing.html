Learning objectives

After completing this topic you will be able to:

  • Name and explain the most common fishing methods in English (trawling, longlining, purse seine, jigging and more)
  • Identify fish species and seafood by their English names
  • Use correct English terms for parts of a fishing vessel
  • Name tools, gear, and safety equipment used on board
  • Translate key vocational words between Norwegian and English
