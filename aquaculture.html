Learning objectives

After completing this topic you will be able to:

  • Describe daily tasks on a fish farm in English (feeding, checking cages, removing morts and more)
  • Name the safety gear and equipment used in aquaculture in English
  • Identify fish species, biology terms, and health issues by their English names
  • Use correct English terms for fish farm structures and equipment
  • Translate key vocational words between Norwegian and English
